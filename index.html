Full Transparency

To provide full transparency, the ridotto protocol runs over multiple smart contracts on decentralized blockchains. Both players & liquidity providers get access to the winning rate, probabilities, and all other aspects hidden by traditional casino operators

Nested Interoperability

The Ridotto is designed to be a cross-chain protocol to optimize smart contracts costs & transactions fees. The protocol's unique approach is to provide an efficient user experience.

Easy build

The protocol enables users to develop unique games on its platform using the Ridotto Lab. It offers robust tools, SDK, libraries & the integration of extra layers as oracles. .

Community governance

Ridotto is community-driven. As a token holder, you will play a critical role in each evolution, proposal, decision, and everything in between. We will work together and combine our ideas to get the best outcome .

All-in-one App

Ridotto’s platform is an all-in-one app that provides a vibrant and entertaining gaming experience. Its user-friendly interface gives users easy access to their favorite games, pools & wallet. .

Staking Rewards

RIDOTTO deviates from the traditional “the house always wins” approach by bringing users into the fold. Users can earn rewards by staking and providing liquidity to the RIDOTTO protocol. This approach provides users with the possibility of becoming the house. .
